LOCATION

Sparkx Arts is headquartered in Owensboro West Village, a diverse neighborhood known for its rich cultural heritage and world-renowned restaurants. In Owensboro, our

office sits in the heart of the Irish capital, steps away from cultural landmarks such as Owensboro river front, Christchurch, and St. Patrick's Cathedral. SparkxArts is

located in downtown Owensboro, around the corner from gourmet food trucks, green spaces, and an array of cultural offerings. So no matter which location you're

working from, you'll have an abundance of activities to keep you excited and entertained.
